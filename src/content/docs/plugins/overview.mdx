## Supercharge Your Behavior Tree

Extend your toolkit with these community-developed plugins. Each one adds unique capabilities to solve common challenges and speed up your workflow.

<Aside type="tip" title="Completely Optional">
  The plugins listed here are not required for the core system to function. Feel free to use them as needed to augment your project!
</Aside>

<Aside type="caution" title="Future Package Structure">
  Currently, all plugins are included directly within the main package. In the future, they may be separated into individual sub-packages to improve modularity. <a href="/ND_BehaviorTreeDocumentPage/contact/">Share your feedback on this proposal!</a>
</Aside>
